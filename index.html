Brief Intro about our Organization

Shashi Dream Foundation is a volunteering group which works extensively for underprivileged children with a motive to educate them and provide them a better life. It has organized various events for inspiring students to work hard and reach their goals ultimately. We are nurturing the children by supporting them and devising methods to have a better education system.
