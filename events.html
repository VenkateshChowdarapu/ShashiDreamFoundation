Empowering Generation Next...

1.Stationary distribution drive

We all know how important writing is. It gives you your world of freedom. You can shape any world any sentence the way you want. Using Sketches, pencils, etc you can draw the world enumerating your creativity. But What do you think, do underprivileged children feel this freedom?
SDF team initiated the mission to distribute stationery to these children. It will add wings to their thoughts. First, SDF distributed stationery to children who come to Shashi Pathshala. The programme went well and impressed by this; nearby schools also approached the SDF team to distribute stationery items in their school. The first event held at Z P School on 1 July 2019. Here, surprisingly the ratio of students was very less, despite having good infrastructure. School authorities told these little kid’s families usually do small scale work like daily wage working, farming. They hardly manage to afford amenities for their children. Therefore, most of them even prefer not to send them to school. The programme was attended by the Headmaster of school, who himself is a social activist. He appreciated the SDF team’s work. Consequently, we reached other schools also to contribute our part to their education.

4.Grocery distribution drive

While the world struggling with the worst pandemic of the century, there is a section of society who is facing one more calamity i.e. hunger. The world is under lockdown, no work is taking place, no livelihood can be earned. People like you and me can afford this lockdown as we get salaries and we do have enough stock of food which can sustain us for months. But for people whose hearth lit only when he earns money for that day. For how many days will they sleep empty stomach? Still, they can but it will be a heart-wrenching situation for them to see their kids with no food for days. For them, this lockdown situation is not less than hell, where they will starve to death. For them, COVID-19 is not scarier but hunger is. By realizing the need of the hour, the govt. India is taking initiatives to take care of their food and money. But will the govt. be able to sustain these people (22% population of India) along with dealing emergency of COVID-19? Here, the nation needs us, these people require our help.

This time also, with no surprise SDF team came forward to take the responsibility. As this task require way more efforts and funds, SDF collaborated with Mitti Ke Rang NGO to cover this drive-in Katol. The aim was to transfer money to their accounts and help them with daily food necessities. SDF reached 1000 families, who were daily wage workers, maids, labourers, vendors, construction workers, etc. Their livelihood was earned on a daily earning basis. SDF team arranged a fund for groceries like rice, sugar, etc. for these people. The team with abiding guidelines of WHO and govt., wearing face masks, and hand sanitized packed their bags to reach these people as Lifesavers. Successfully, On 4th April 2020, the team was able to accomplish its mission. They distributed the groceries to the families which can help them to sustain for about 21 days. SDF team also make them aware of the situation of the country and ask them to not risk their lives. SDF team will be always available whenever they require any help. SDF team also ask them to take precautionary measure to fight against this pandemic and in any health emergency, they must seek medical help.

About the Exam

To make an effort in the direction to help children grow, the Shashi Dream Foundation team came up with an idea to organize quiz in different schools and at a different level. The motive of the team behind organizing quiz was to make this young generation realize the importance of textbooks they are studying. These books are not just a compilation of paper rather they are an elixir for their bright future.

Now the question in front of them was where to organize? They chose three schools which do not come under the City Council. The schools were Keshav Rao Pawar School, Government School, and Katol High School. The SDF team met school authorities and proposed their idea to organize a quiz with the help of their team and everything from quiz paper to pen will be arranged by SDF only. And school authorities gave a positive response to the proposal.
