ABOUT US

When the journey started?

The journey of Shashi Dream Foundation (SDF) hasn’t started all of a sudden as a miracle but this foundation is an outcome of someone’s years of hard- work and dedication, his effort to pay tribute to his mother, and patriotism to do something for his nation. The boy started his journey from a small-town area situated in a district of Nagpur, in Maharashtra. He worked hard and cleared the IIT exam and admitted to IIT-BHU then after completing engineering he got admitted into IIM and secured a good job. For some middle-class people, all these achievements are just above perfection and satisfaction he will not wish to think beyond it. But this person had a zeal to do more something that can impact society. This person is Mr. Harshal Dhoke, no other than a common man with a big and a brave heart.

SDF got its name

Mr. Harshal Dhoke wanted to give this foundation the strongest bond of himself and for him, his mother bond was impregnable. So, to personify the love that the mother has with her children he named this NGO on his mother’s name Shashi. From there the dream started to come true of Shashi Dream Foundation.

Why Katol to start with?

Someone has said “Start where you are. Use what you have. Do what you can.” For Harshal it’s quite apt. He has seen the miseries of the people of his area. So, before laying bricks on some alien land he thought to start from his land. Therefore, he chose his native place Katol to start from.

Hurdles!

As already told making SDF was not that much easy as it seems to narrate. Any institution cannot be run by only one person. Harshal had to make a long journey. The major problem came into his path was convincing children parent’s. “Kya karenge jyada padh likh kr”, “bhau ye log sarkari school me jate hn aur kya pdhana hai”, “ladki hai ghar ka kaam sikh lene do saab”, these were sentences he had to listen at every door. Arranging funds to run SDF in itself was a challenge, aligning staff at minimum salary was also not an easy task.
But after facing all these problems SDF has managed to balance the system. Today there is a healthy number of children attending Shashi Pathshala, the teaching staff is also considerable and yes, the work is full-on progress


What's next:

Still, it’s a long expedition to complete. SDF dreams to expand in every corner of India to help needy and make education more accessible to everyone with no partiality on any basis. SDF for near future dreaming to build a big school with every basic facility in it. It will help poor children to study in it, stay here eat so that they can be grown in a healthy environment and get ready for their future.
SDF believes no one deserves a neglected life. It focusses on Child Protection, Education and Maternal Health. And see the future with value-based communities and dignified and informed citizens. So, are you ready to become part of this journey?
